Welcome to Hutsle Lawn and Landscape LLC

We are a professional landscaping company based in Douglassville, Pennsylvania. Our expert team, led by owner Jude Segaline, is dedicated to providing quality lawn and landscape services to enhance the beauty and health of your outdoor space.

Our Services

  • Leaf Cleanup: Efficient removal of fallen leaves to keep your yard tidy and healthy.
  • Lawn Mowing: Regular lawn mowing to maintain the appearance of your lawn.
  • Hardscaping: Design and installation of patios, walkways, retaining walls, and other hardscape features.
  • Mulching: Application of mulch to improve soil health and keep your garden looking great.
  • Weed Control: Effective weed management to keep your lawn and garden weed-free.
  • Bush Trimming: Regular trimming of bushes and shrubs for a neat and healthy garden.

All prices vary based on the size and scope of the work required. Please contact us for a custom quote.
